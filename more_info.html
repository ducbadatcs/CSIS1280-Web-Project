About Chocolat

Welcome to Chocoby, where the art of chocolate meets the heart of artisanal expertise, our chocolate atelier is a place where the rich tapestry of flavor is woven into every bar and bonbon. We are more than chocolatiers; we are a family of passionate artisans devoted to creating the most exquisite handmade chocolate experiences.

Our Special Touch:

What distinguishes us is our dedication to quality and authenticity. Each chocolate piece is handcrafted from bean to bar using the finest sustainably sourced cocoa, joined with a selection of the best local and exotic ingredients.

Our Why:

Our mission is to carry joy through the medium of chocolate, crafting each one as a work of art to be savored. We honor the chocolate-making traditions while bathing them with our innovative flavours, always keeping the delight of our customers in mind.
